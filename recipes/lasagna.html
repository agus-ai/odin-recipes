Spinach Lasagna

A spinach lasagna recipe made with fresh spinach and plenty of cheese.

An esay recipe of a vegetarian lasagna with diferent kind of cheese, spinach and pre-made pasta. 1 hour and 35 minutes is the total preparation time. Up to 12 serving can be made of it, although it isn's decreed.

Ingredients

  • 15 lasagna noodles
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 1 cup of chopped fresh mushrooms
  • 1 cup of chopped onions
  • 1 tablespoon of minced garlic
  • 2 cups of fresh spinach
  • 3 cups of ricotta cheese
  • 2/3 cup of grated Romano cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of dired oregano
  • 1 teaspoon of dried basil leaves
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 3 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 3 cups of tomato pasta sauce
  • 1 cup of grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Step 1

    2. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

  2. Step 2

    3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to boil. Add lasagna noodles and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.

  3. Step 3

    4. Meanwhile, heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat. Add mushrooms, onions, and garlic and cook until onions are tender; drain excess liquid and cool.

  4. Step 4

    5. At the same time, bring a small pot of water to a boil; add spinach and simmer for 5 minutes. Drain and cool slightly; squeeze out any excess liquid and finely chop.

  5. Step 5

    6. Combine ricotta cheese, Romano cheese, egg, salt, oregano, basil, and pepper in a bowl. Add cooled mushroom mixture and spinach; beat with an electric mixer on low speed for 1 minute.

  6. Step 6

    7. Lay 5 lasagna noodles in the bottom of a 9x13-inch backing dish. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta-spinach mixture over noodles, then sprinkle with 1 cup of mozzarella cheese and 1/3 cup Parmesan cheese. Spread 1 cup pasta sauce over top. Repeat layers two more times, the cover with aluminum foil.

  7. Step 7

    8. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour. Cool 15 minutes before serving.
